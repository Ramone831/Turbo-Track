Our Story

Turbo Tracks was founded in *2009* in Kingston, Jamaica, by a group of RC racing enthusiasts who wanted to bring high-quality remote-controlled vehicles to the Caribbean. What started as a small hobby shop has grown into *Jamaica's leading RC car retailer and event organizer*.

Over the years, we have partnered with top brands, expanded our inventory to include high-performance RC cars, and hosted *some of the biggest RC racing events in the region*.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide top-quality RC vehicles and accessories while fostering a strong, passionate RC racing community.

Vision: To become the #1 RC car retailer in the Caribbean, offering the best products and hosting premier RC racing events.
